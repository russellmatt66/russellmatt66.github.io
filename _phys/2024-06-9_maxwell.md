### Maxwell's Equations in Vacuum 

$$
\begin{align}
\div \vec{E} &= \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_{0}} \\
\curl \vec{B} &= \mu_{0}\vec{J} + \frac{1}{c^{2}}\pdv{\vec{E}}{}{t} \\
\curl \vec{E} &= -\pdv{\vec{B}}{}{t} \\
\div \vec{B} &= 0 
\end{align}
$$
